Mera Bazaar (Inspired from CornaVirus and Lockdown)

Mera Bazaar is an Indian e-commerce company based in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was founded by Vineet Tripathi in 2020. The company initially focused on grocery sales, before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home essentials & groceries, and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Grofer's, and Modicare.As of April 2020, Mera Bazaar held a 0.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry.Mera Bazaar is significantly dominant in the sale of apparel (a position that was bolstered by its acquisitions of Myntra and Jabong.com), and was described as being "neck and neck" with Grofer's in the sale of Grocery Products. Mera Bazaar is inspired from 2020 CoronaVirus outbreak and wants to help people in need of food and essential consumer goods.
